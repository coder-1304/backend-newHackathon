Welcome to the Project Management Tool

Streamline your projects and collaborate with your team effectively.

Our project management tool is a powerful software designed to streamline project planning, execution, and collaboration. With a range of intuitive features and robust functionality, our tool helps teams stay organized, meet deadlines, and achieve project success

Features

Collaboration

Project managers will be able to collaborate with others to contribute in their project. It includes Searching people , accepting requests and file sharing.

Progress check

The project progress could be checked by whether the tasks is completed by individual person or not. The task list consists of To dos , Inprogress , Review , Done options to track.

Discussion

Discussion feature allows people to share files and comment on the people's tasks uploaded. Also file can be downloaded.
